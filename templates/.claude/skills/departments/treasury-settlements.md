# Treasury And Settlements Skill

Load this skill for cash movement, bank statements, FX, payments, settlements, SSI, SWIFT messages, or liquidity workflows.

## Primary Concerns

- Payment initiation, approval, release, acknowledgement, and repair.
- Bank/custodian statements, cash balances, FX confirmations, and settlement status.
- SWIFT MT/MX messages, bank proprietary files, and payment rails.
- SSI, account, counterparty, currency, calendar, and cut-off rules.
- Cash forecasting, overdraft, liquidity, and failed settlement handling.

## Read First

- `.claude/references/vendor-file-intake.md`
- `.claude/references/external-systems.md`
- `.claude/references/gotchas.md`

## Ask Before Changing

- Payment formatting, approval controls, SSI, settlement finality, or bank cut-off behavior is unclear.
- The change affects real cash movement or external settlement instructions.
