# Fund Accounting Skill

Load this skill for NAV, fund books, accounting ledger, accruals, expenses, prices, corporate actions, valuation, or administrator files.

## Primary Concerns

- NAV calculation and validation.
- Fund, share class, portfolio, ledger, and account hierarchy.
- Pricing hierarchy, stale price handling, fair value, and tolerance checks.
- Accruals, fees, income, expenses, and corporate action postings.
- Administrator/custodian files from State Street, SS&C, BNY, Citi, JPMorgan, Northern Trust, and related platforms.

## Ask Before Changing

- NAV, pricing, accounting, or ledger behavior is not backed by acceptance criteria or source documentation.
- Tolerances, rounding, or accounting date rules are unclear.
- Existing code disagrees with vendor/admin documentation.
