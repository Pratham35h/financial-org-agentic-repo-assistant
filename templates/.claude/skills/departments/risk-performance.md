# Risk And Performance Skill

Load this skill for performance returns, attribution, benchmark data, exposures, factor models, stress testing, risk metrics, or analytics files.

## Primary Concerns

- Performance calculation basis, benchmark selection, and return methodology.
- Attribution, exposure, factor, VaR, stress, and scenario data.
- Data from MSCI/Barra, Bloomberg, Refinitiv/LSEG, Morningstar, S&P, ICE, index providers, and internal analytics platforms.
- Historical restatement, effective dates, and benchmark/security mapping.
- Reporting consistency across portfolio, benchmark, fund, and account hierarchies.

## Ask Before Changing

- Methodology, benchmark, date basis, or restatement handling is unclear.
- The change affects published performance, client reporting, or regulatory disclosures.
