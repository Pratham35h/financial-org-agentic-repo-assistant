# Compliance And Regulatory Skill

Load this skill for regulatory reports, surveillance, restrictions, sanctions, audit evidence, retention, privacy, or controlled data.

## Primary Concerns

- Regulatory reporting completeness, lineage, and attestation.
- Trade surveillance, investment restrictions, and breach workflows.
- Sanctions/watchlist/reference data feeds where applicable.
- PII, investor data, material non-public information, and licensed market data controls.
- Audit trails, retention, approval, and evidence preservation.

- `.claude/references/vendor-file-intake.md`
- `.claude/references/external-systems.md`
- `.claude/references/gotchas.md`

## Ask Before Changing

- Regulatory interpretation, retention period, approval workflow, or report lineage is unclear.
- The change alters reported values, suppresses alerts, or changes audit evidence.
